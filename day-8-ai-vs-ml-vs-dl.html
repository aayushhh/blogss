Aayush March 6, 2021 Artificial Intelligence

AI vs ML vs DL! How are they different?

👉 AI - Machines that can learn, reason, and act for themselves

👉 ML - Looking for patterns in data using statistical algorithms

👉 DL - Technique to perform ML inspired by our brain's network of neurons
